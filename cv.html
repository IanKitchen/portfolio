Summary

I have over 10 years of experience designing software in a complex and demanding environment. I take pride in the creation of highly usable and effective interfaces. I have designed many experiences ranging from workflow frameworks to advanced search tools to highly interactive data visualisations.

It is my belief that great software comes from a deep understanding of user's goals and the environment in which they work. Once an understanding is created I'll use a variety of techniques to explore the solution space, prototype and evaluate. I have very strong Axure skills, having built some very sophisticated prototypes, but I'm happy using Balsamiq or Javascript too.

The software systems I've designed meet the needs of several personas in a customer base spanning four major verticals. I have worked with teams using Agile and Waterfall methodologies, in large teams and alone.

I can contribute both creative problem solving skills along with an engineering discipline to writing specifications where appropriate.
